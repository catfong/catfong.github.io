Research

Systems ecology for resilience and sustainability

I work across marine and terrestrial systems to understand how ecological and social systems respond to change. From trait-based frameworks for seaweed and aquaculture to wildfire resilience and sustainable food production, my research focuses on building resilience at multiple scales—and on partnerships that connect science to practice.
